How We Work

Discovery

We engage with you to understand what your problems are, what you’re looking to build and ensure the platform we deliver is right for you.

Architectural Design

We diagram and plan out your cloud architecture to ensure it covers all your bases and will meet your needs for many years to come.

Implementation

We write out your infrastructure platform in Terraform (the Infrastructure as Code tool that we’re masters of). This is central to driving a visible, consistent, and automated platform that will be easy to maintain.

Production

We get your infrastructure platform live in production so it’s serving real customers and we can ensure you’re happy with the finished product.

Support & Maintenance

We have gotten your platform off the ground and now we’re ready to either train your engineers to take over or keep it running for you as you see fit.
